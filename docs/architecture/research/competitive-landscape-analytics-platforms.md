**Research Area:** Multi-domain analytics and business intelligence platforms

## 2. Detailed Competitive Analysis

### 2.1 Databox

**Strengths:**

- 70+ native integrations across marketing, sales, and e-commerce
- Custom metric builder for calculated metrics
- Template library for quick setup
- White-label dashboard options
- Strong mobile app

**Weaknesses:**

- Limited AI-generated insights
- Template-based approach limits flexibility
- Reporting features less sophisticated than dedicated tools
- No agency partner model beyond white-label

**Pricing Model:**

- Freemium with 3 data sources
- Paid tiers based on data source count
- Annual billing discount of ~20%

### 2.2 AgencyAnalytics

**Strengths:**

- Agency-first positioning
- White-label reports and dashboards
- Automated client reporting workflows
- 60+ integrations
- Client management features

**Weaknesses:**

- Marketing-focused only (no finance/operations)
- Limited AI capabilities
- Rigid report templates
- No multi-domain intelligence

**Pricing Model:**

- Per-client pricing ($49-$399/client/month)
- Agency discounts for volume
- White-label available on higher tiers

### 2.3 Klipfolio

**Strengths:**

- Highly customizable dashboards
- 100+ integrations
- Power user features (custom KLIPs, calculated metrics)
- Strong scheduling and alerts
- Embedded analytics options

**Weaknesses:**

- Steep learning curve
- Limited template library
- No agency-specific features
- Minimal AI capabilities
- Manual metric building required

**Pricing Model:**

- Per-dashboard pricing
- Tiered by features and data source count
- Agency volume pricing available

### 2.4 Google Looker Studio

**Strengths:**

- Free to use
- Google ecosystem integration (GA4, GSC, Ads)
- Community template gallery
- Embedding capabilities
- Data blending

**Weaknesses:**

- Google data sources work best
- No native AI insights
- Limited delivery automation
- No multi-tenant management
- Manual configuration required

**Pricing Model:**

- Free for individuals
- Paid for embedding in commercial products

## 5. Pricing Strategy Insights

### 5.1 Market Pricing Patterns

**Entry Level:** $49-$99/month (5-10 data sources)
**Mid-Market:** $149-$399/month (15-25 data sources)
**Enterprise:** $500-$2,000/month (unlimited data sources)
**Agency Per-Client:** $29-$99/client/month

### 5.2 Pricing Psychology

**What Works:**

- Anchoring with a "Pro" tier at mid-market price
- Feature differentiation beyond data source count
- Annual billing with visible savings
- Free trial or freemium for PLG

**What Avoids:**

- Complex usage calculations
- Hidden fees or surprise overages
- Feature confusion across tiers
- Arbitrary limits that block value

## 7. Recommendations

### 7.1 Competitive Positioning

1. **Lean into AI differentiation** — No competitor has native, actionable insights as a core feature
2. **Emphasize multi-domain** — First platform to truly span marketing, finance, and operations
3. **Target agencies early** — Less competition in agency-native features
4. **PLG with templates** — Reduce friction with pre-built configurations

### 7.2 Feature Prioritization

**Must-Have (MVP):**

- Core marketing integrations (GA4, Meta, TikTok)
- AI-generated insights and recommendations
- Template library for common insight types
- Agency multi-client management
- Flexible metric selection per insight

**Differentiators (Phase 2):**

- Finance connectors (QuickBooks, Stripe)
- Cross-domain insights (marketing impact on finance)
- Advanced AI customization (model selection, parameters)
- White-label reporting

**Expansion (Phase 3):**

- Operations connectors (CRM, project management)
- Predictive analytics
- Custom LLM fine-tuning per domain

### 7.3 Pricing Strategy

**Launch Pricing (Recommended):**

- **Starter:** $79/month (up to 5 connectors, 3 insights)
- **Professional:** $199/month (up to 15 connectors, unlimited insights)
- **Agency:** $299/month + $39/client/month
- **Enterprise:** Custom pricing

**Rationale:**

- Premium to freemium competitors due to AI value
- Competitive with mid-market tiers
- Agency pricing per client is market-aligned
- Room for annual billing discount (15-20%)
